Outside of work, fun is the name of the game. Here are some of my interest.

Gaming

Since I can remember, I've always loved video games. The first console I owned was the Nintendo64. Since then each time a newer generation of console came out, I would always try to save up enough money to buy them.

Whenever I have time I'm usually playing on my Playstation. I also enjoy playing games on my Switch and on my PC. Although I always fall back to my trusty Playstation.

I enjoy playing every genre of video games such as JRPG's, FPS', platformers, puzzles, open world adventure and even driving games.

Whenever I can find time to myself, you'll probably find me sitting infront of a screen, with a headset on and a controller in hand.

Shows

Going to see shows is another thing I enjoy. It's always a great experience going out to see what artist can do, be it a concert or a festival.

When it comes to concerts I've seen every genre of music you can think of. I personally love metal shows and edm shows. With those genres I feel like I can actually get into the music more than anything else.

On the other hand, when I want to just sit back and relax, orchestras are the way to go. Seeing the conductor lead the musicians to a harmonious sound brings a smile to my face. It's always fun to even see special orchestras such as when they play music from a popular movie or video game.

With the dogs we love training and going out with them. Our dogs love to go to parks and forest trails. Our Boarder Collie especially love to do obstecal courses.

Our cats on the otherhand prefer to stay inside and sleep all day. When we're feeling lazy and just wanna stay in bed our cats are already there waiing. They're always ready to cuddle up and dream away.
